HISTORY

The first meeting of Eunoia Nutrition was held in Hollywood, California, USA on January 19, 2021. Rachel attended a Gamblers Anonymous meeting two years earlier to help a friend and realized the format of the Twelve Steps meeting might hold an answer to her own addictive behavior as it related to food. Her vision of how this simple program could bring recovery to all those who suffered from compulsive overeating resulted in the worldwide organization that exists today.
